Understanding the complexity of E-commerce website development

Designing a user-friendly, secure e-commerce site requires advanced knowledge and expertise. While there are many pre-made shopping cart systems available, most of them demand knowledge of the programming language for even the simplest of changes to be possible. You may buy a shopping cart system thinking you’ll just tweak it a little, only to find out it was designed in such a way that modifications are challenging at the least and impossible for those who are not technically savvy

E-commerce sites have varying needs. Your site may need more or less complex settings and features than another site. We at IndexIdeas.com understand the ins and outs of e-commerce design and Internet business. We take the time to discover what the needs will be for your online store and tailor your e-commerce storefront accordingly.

Common specifications of e-commerce websites:

  • Integrated content management system
  • Access to changing product photos, descriptions, and prices
  • Secure financial transactions
  • One click ordering capability
  • Graphical sales reporting
  • Usability statistics
  • Warehouse tracking
  • Order tracking
  • Wish list management
  • Order history management
  • Cross platform solutions

Whatever your e-commerce needs, we can develop a site that works the way you want it. Don’t mess around with clunky pre-made e-commerce applications that don’t deliver the site you want. Let us tailor our site exactly as you imagined it.
