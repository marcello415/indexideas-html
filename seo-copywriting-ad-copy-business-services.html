Launching an Email Campaign?

More and more businesses are launching email campaigns because email is an inexpensive, efficient way to reach your customers when they are home, relaxed, and ready to receive your materials. Make sure your email campaigns are well-designed, since there’s nothing more irritating than opening an email and feeling like you’ve just been spammed.

Here are some suggestions for a successful email campaign:

  • Make sure your emails are welcome. Always get permission from customers when you gather their contact information and provide an easy opt-out so they can opt not to receive your ads if they wish. You want to entice your customers to use your products or services, not annoy them until they can’t stand you.
  • Make your email informative, entertaining, or useful. Do not just spam out ads to your customers. If you write an email that is somehow of use to the recipient, you increase your chances of them reading your emails and add value to your advertising. Many businesses do this by sending out newsletters or interactive emails that double as online games or surveys.
  • Limit the number of emails you send out. Don’t irritate your customers!
  • Include coupons, information about sales, or some sort of incentive in every email. You need to give the customer a valid reason to read your emails.

Our copywriters know how to write effective direct marketing copy for email campaigns, employing either a soft or hard sell, as you see appropriate. Our expert copywriters will communicate with you so they understand the tone and message you want to convey
